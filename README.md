Privacy Policy: Human Relationship App
1. Introduction
The "Human Relationship App" is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled. Unlike traditional CRM tools, our app is designed with a "Local-First" and "Privacy-First" approach.
2. Data We Collect and Why
•
Contacts: The app requests access to your device contacts only when you initiate an "Import." This data (names, phone numbers, emails) is stored in a secure local database to help you manage your relationships.
•
Call Logs & Phone State: The app monitors call activity to provide "Quick Add" prompts for unknown numbers and "Follow-up" reminders after important calls.
•
Interaction History: Notes and summaries of your meetings or messages are created by you and stored securely.
3. Local Encryption (Security)
All your relationship data is stored in a military-grade encrypted database (SQLCipher) on your physical device. We do not have a central server, and we cannot "see" or access your data. Your database is protected by a unique device key and an optional backup password.
4. Third-Party AI Processing
To provide intelligent insights (Nudges, Deep Persona, Advisor Chat), the app sends specific relationship data (like names and notes) to the AI Provider you have configured (e.g., Google Gemini, OpenAI).
•
This data is sent only when you request an AI feature.
•
The data is handled according to the Privacy Policy of the provider you choose.
•
You use your own API Key, ensuring you remain in control of your data relationship with the AI provider.
5. Data Sharing
We never sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your data stays on your device and only travels to the AI provider you specifically authorize.
6. Backups
If you use the "Export Backup" feature, an encrypted file is created. You are responsible for the security of this file and the password you set for it.
7. Your Rights
You have full control over your data. You can delete all data at any time using the "Clear Local Data" button in the Settings menu.
